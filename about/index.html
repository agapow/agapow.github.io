About this site

There’s been complaints that this website was overly cryptic about who I am. To that end, a thumbnail biography follows:

  • There’s only one Paul / Paul-Michael / P-M Agapow in the world. If you’re looking for that name, it’s me and this is my personal website. If you’re looking for another Agapow, they may be in Australia, Canada, the US, France or Russia.
  • I'm from Australia, although many years removed at this point. I was brought up in Brisbane, studied in Melbourne and now live & work in London.
  • I do biomedical research for a living (biomedical data science, precision medicine, translational bioinformatics, etc.), although I used to do research on disease, macroevolution & conservation. I’ve published on this, given some talks and written some associated software.
  • According to an industry blogger, I'm "charismatic and consciously antagonistic". In Italy, I’m apparently “a repository of video art on the border between performance, work and virtuality”.
  • The last time I looked, I had an Erdos number of 4.
